Spezhe is a voice-first macOS app that turns speech to text anywhere you type. Trigger dictation with a single hotkey and keep your focus while your words appear instantly.

WHY SPEZHE?

Spezhe is built for daily work. It stays lightweight, works across apps, and keeps your data on-device by default.

FEATURES

- Global Hotkey - Start and stop dictation from any app
- App-Wide Capture - Use Spezhe in Mail, Messages, Notes, and more
- Smart Punctuation - Accurate transcription powered by state-of-the-art models
- Floating Dictation UI - Keep controls close without switching windows
- History & Replay - Review recent transcripts and replay audio for quick edits
- Metadata Insights - See duration, app, and time for each session

PERFECT FOR

- Writing emails, documents, and notes faster
- Brain dumping ideas without breaking flow
- Capturing meeting summaries and quick drafts
- Anyone who wants less typing and more thinking

YOUR DATA, YOUR PRIVACY

Spezhe runs locally and does not send your audio or transcription data to our servers by default.
